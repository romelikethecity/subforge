Frequently Asked Questions

What trades do you cover?

We segment by 16 major commercial trade categories: general contractors, mechanical/HVAC, electrical, plumbing, concrete, structural steel, drywall and acoustical, glazing and curtainwall, masonry, commercial roofing, painting and coatings, flooring, fire protection, site work and excavation, demolition, and specialty trades. Each has 5+ subcategories where the data supports it.

How is this different from Dodge or ConstructConnect?

Dodge sells project intelligence to contractors. ConstructConnect sells bid opportunities to contractors. Both products are aimed at the contractor side of the market. SubForge sells contractor contact data to vendors. We tell you who the contractors are, who runs them, and how to reach them. Different product, different buyer.

Can you segment by project type (healthcare, education, multifamily)?

Yes. We classify contractors by project type focus using license history, completed-project records, and firm self-reporting. Filter for healthcare GCs, K-12 specialists, multifamily builders, or any combination of project types. This is one of the highest-signal filters we offer.

Do you include bonding capacity and license class?

Yes. Every contractor record includes the state license number, license class, active status, and bonding capacity tier where available. Bonding data is sourced from public surety filings and state board records.

What data fields are included?

Each record includes the contractor's company name, owner or principal name, verified email, direct phone, license number and class, bonding capacity, primary trade specialization, project type focus, and firm size. Custom fields are available on request.

How long does delivery take?

Most orders are delivered in 3-5 business days. Complex builds with custom criteria or very large geographies may take 5-7 days. Rush delivery is available for time-sensitive projects.

Is there a minimum order?

No minimum. You can order a list for a single metro area or every state. Pricing is per-project, based on the size and complexity of your request.
