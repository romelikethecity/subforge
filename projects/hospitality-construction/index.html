Hospitality Construction Contractor Data

Hotels, resorts, restaurants, and branded hospitality projects. Brand-standard construction means contractors who can deliver to specific PIP requirements from Marriott, Hilton, Hyatt, IHG, and independent operators.

What's included

License-verified contractor contacts at firms with hospitality construction experience. Each record includes the firm name, owner contact, verified email, direct phone, business address, license number with active status, trade classification, and project type focus.

Project type classification is determined by cross-referencing project records, license class history, and firm-reported portfolios. We verify project experience at the time of list build, not from stale directory data.

Why project type matters

Hospitality construction is dominated by contractors with brand-standard experience. PIP compliance, FF&E coordination, and finish-quality requirements push out general commercial GCs.

Frequently Asked Questions

What data do you have for hospitality construction contractors?

Every record contains: contractor name, owner contact, verified email, direct phone, business address, license number, license class, bonding capacity tier, and trade specialization. If you need additional fields like project portfolio or union status, let us know when you place your order.

How do you verify contractor data?

We cross-check every contractor against their state board for active license status, validate emails through deliverability testing, verify phone numbers against carrier databases, and confirm firm details against multiple public sources.

Can I combine project type with trade filters?

Yes. You can request a list filtered by project type and trade simultaneously. For example, mechanical contractors with healthcare project experience in California, or electrical contractors handling data center work in Virginia. Custom combinations are standard.

How do you classify project type focus?

We combine completed-project records, license class history, firm website signals, and self-reported project portfolios. Contractors with broad portfolios are tagged with multiple project types. Commercial vs residential is a separate filter.
