Contractor Data for Trade Associations

Recruit members across trade-specific verticals. Built for companies like ABC, AGC, NECA, MCAA, SMACNA chapter membership recruiting.

The problem

Membership recruiting at trade associations means reaching contractors in specific trade verticals. Your local chapter wants every commercial mechanical contractor in the metro, not a generic construction list with HVAC suppliers and residential remodelers mixed in.

How SubForge solves this

SubForge segments by trade and geography for chapter-level recruiting. NECA chapters get electrical contractors. MCAA chapters get mechanical firms. SMACNA gets sheet metal. Each contact is the owner or principal, who actually decides on membership.

What you get

A clean CSV with every field you need to run targeted campaigns. Firm name, owner contact, verified email, direct phone, business address, state license number with active status, license class, trade classification, and project type focus. No platform to log into. No annual contract to sign.

You tell us your target criteria: trades, project types, geographic territory, firm size, license class. We build the list from state contractor boards and 50+ verified sources. Every record is deliverability-tested before it reaches your inbox.

  • Territory planning with verified contractor locations
  • Cold outreach campaigns with accurate decision-maker contact data
  • Account-based marketing with trade and project type segmentation
  • CRM enrichment with license status and firm details
  • Market sizing and TAM analysis for commercial construction segments

Frequently Asked Questions

What data does SubForge provide for trade associations?

Standard fields: company name, owner name, deliverability-tested email, direct phone, business address, state license number, license class, active status, trade classification, and project type focus. We can add bonding capacity or ENR ranking on request.

How is the data verified?

Every contact starts with a state license record. We verify the contractor is actively licensed, confirm their trade classification, validate the email address, and check the phone number. Records that fail any verification step are flagged or excluded.

How fresh is the data?

Lists are built fresh for each order. We don't sell pre-built databases. When you place an order, we pull the latest license records and run verification at that point. Typical data age at delivery is under 30 days for license status and under 7 days for contact verification.

What trades can I filter by?

We segment by 16 major commercial trade categories: general contractors, mechanical/HVAC, electrical, plumbing, concrete, structural steel, drywall and acoustical, glazing and curtainwall, masonry, commercial roofing, painting and coatings, flooring, fire protection, site work and excavation, demolition, and specialty trades. Each has 5+ subcategories where the data supports it.

How long does delivery take?

Most orders are delivered in 3-5 business days. Complex builds with custom criteria may take 5-7 days. Rush delivery is available for time-sensitive campaigns.
