Contractor Data for Construction Software

Reach commercial GCs and trades evaluating new platforms. Built for companies like Procore, Buildertrend, PlanGrid, Autodesk Construction Cloud, Trimble.

The problem

Your TAM is roughly 1.2 million licensed commercial contractors. ZoomInfo lumps them all under construction NAICS codes with no way to distinguish a $200M mechanical contractor from a residential remodeler. Your SDRs spend their day filtering contacts that should never have been in the pipeline.

How SubForge solves this

SubForge segments by trade specialization, project type focus, license class, and commercial vs residential mix. Target the VP of Operations at a commercial mechanical contractor running healthcare projects. Reach the IT director at a $100M GC managing K-12 work. Every contact is license-verified and cross-checked against project records.

What you get

A clean CSV with every field you need to run targeted campaigns. Firm name, owner contact, verified email, direct phone, business address, state license number with active status, license class, trade classification, and project type focus. No platform to log into. No annual contract to sign.

You tell us your target criteria: trades, project types, geographic territory, firm size, license class. We build the list from state contractor boards and 50+ verified sources. Every record is deliverability-tested before it reaches your inbox.

Frequently Asked Questions

What data does SubForge provide for construction software?

Every record contains: contractor name, owner contact, verified email, direct phone, business address, license number, license class, bonding capacity tier, and trade specialization. If you need additional fields like project portfolio or union status, let us know when you place your order.

How is the data verified?

We cross-check every contractor against their state board for active license status, validate emails through deliverability testing, verify phone numbers against carrier databases, and confirm firm details against multiple public sources.

How fresh is the data?

Lists are built fresh for each order. We don't sell pre-built databases. When you place an order, we pull the latest license records and run verification at that point. Typical data age at delivery is under 30 days for license status and under 7 days for contact verification.

What trades can I filter by?

We segment by 16 major commercial trade categories: general contractors, mechanical/HVAC, electrical, plumbing, concrete, structural steel, drywall and acoustical, glazing and curtainwall, masonry, commercial roofing, painting and coatings, flooring, fire protection, site work and excavation, demolition, and specialty trades. Each has 5+ subcategories where the data supports it.

How long does delivery take?

Most orders are delivered in 3-5 business days. Complex builds with custom criteria may take 5-7 days. Rush delivery is available for time-sensitive campaigns.
