Pricing FAQ

What's included in each record?

Every record includes contractor name, owner contact, verified email, direct phone, business address, license number, license class, bonding capacity tier, trade specialization, and project type focus. Growth plans add commercial vs residential classification and ENR ranking where applicable.

Can I get a sample before purchasing?

Yes. We provide a free 20-record sample matched to your target trade and geography so you can evaluate data quality before committing to a purchase.

Do you offer email and mobile enrichment?

Email and direct phone are standard in every record. Mobile phone enrichment is available as an add-on for contacts where a mobile number is available.

How fast can I get my data?

Most orders ship within 3-5 business days. Free samples take 2-3 days. Rush delivery is available for time-sensitive projects.

Is there a minimum order?

No minimum. You can order a list for a single metro area, a state, or the entire country. Pricing is per-project based on record count and complexity.

Do any services require annual contracts?

No. Contact data is pay-per-order. Ongoing builds are month-to-month. No annual commitments on any product.
